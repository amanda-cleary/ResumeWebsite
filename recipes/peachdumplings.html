Recipe

Peach Croissant Dumplings

Inspired by this recipe for Peach Dumpings.

Prep time: 20 minutes

Bake time: 35-40 minutes

Yield: 16 "dumplings"

These "dumplings" are perfect with a side dish of vanilla ice cream. Serve warm and savor the sweet, fruity flavors!

Ingredients

  • 2 packages of Pillsbury dough crescent rolls (8 count each)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 can of peaches (half-sliced)

Directions

  1. Begin preheating the oven to 350°F. Melt butter (1/2 cup)in a small saucepan over low heat. Mix in the brown sugar (1/2 cup). If desired, add cinnamon for extra flavor.
  2. Meanwhile, roll 1/4 to 1/2 of a peach into each triangle of the crescent rolls. Mix in leftover peach juice with the butter and brown sugar.
  3. Line an oven-safe baking dish with the croissant dumplings. They should fit as snug as possible.
  4. Pour the mixture over the dumplings. It should fill in all cracks and soak into the dough. Optionally, sprinkle additional brown sugar over top.
  5. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the dumplings are golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  6. Allow to cool for at least 10 minutes before serving.
