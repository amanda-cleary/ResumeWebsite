Cake Mix Cookies

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 35 minutes

Yield: 15-20 cookies

Inspired by my college roommate's baking experimentations, these cookies are soft, chewy, easy to make, and wonderfully customizable. Favorites include strawberry cake mix with white chocolate chips and rainbow sprinkles, and lemon cake mix with vanilla icing.

Ingredients

  • 1 box any flavor cake mix
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup oil

Optional Add-ins

  • 1 cup (white) chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup sprinkles
  • 1/4 cup icing

Directions

  1. Combine cake mix, eggs, and oil in a large bowl.
  2. Roll into balls and place on a baking sheet covered with parchment paper.
  3. Optionally, press a few sprinkles or chocolate chips into the top of each cookie.
  4. Bake at 350°F for about 10 minutes.
  5. Allow to cool before optionally adding icing to the tops of each cookie.
