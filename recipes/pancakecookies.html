Almond Banana (Pancake) Cookies

Inspired by this recipe for Cherry Almond Cookies.

Prep time: 10 minutes

Cook time: 15 minutes

Serves: 2 to 3

More pancake than cookie, these baked goods are perfect for an on-the-go breakfast treat.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs (optionally, reduce to 1)
  • 1 tsp pure almond extract (double for more flavor)
  • 1 tsp lemon juicer
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 mashed ripe bananas
  • 2ish cups flour

Directions

  1. Slice the bananas into small chunks. Preheat oven to 350°F. Prepare two sheet pans and set aside.
  2. In a medium to large bowl, melt the butter (1/2 cup). Add both sugars (3/4 cup granulated and 1/4 cup brown) and whisk until fully incorporated.
  3. Add the egg, almond extract (1 tsp), and lemon juice (1 tsp). Whisk until the mixture is combined and smooth.
  4. Sprinkle the baking soda (1/2 tsp) and salt (1/2 tsp) over the mixture and stir well. Then add the flour (1.5 cups) and stir. Fold in the chopped bananas.
  5. Let chill in freezer or refrigerator to reduce expansion during baking.
  6. Use about 1 tbsp of dough for each cookie, and leave plenty of space around each, as they will expan significantly while baking.
  7. Bake for about 12 minutes, until edges and bottoms are browned. Allow the cookies to cool on the pan before removing them.
